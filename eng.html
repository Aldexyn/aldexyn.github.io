About Us


Aldexyn is a private equity firm established by seasoned professionals with extensive experience in private equity and investment banking. We do not just deploy capital but offer a comprehensive suite of services tailored to the needs of both portfolio companies and non-portfolio companies.

The CEO has a proven track record in M&A advisory, having previously held positions at leading institutions such as Nomura Securities, Merrill Lynch Japan Securities, and CPP Investments.






Private Equity


We differentiate ourselves from traditional private equity funds by prioritizing the long-term success of our investee companies and their stakeholders. Unlike time-bound fund structures, we take an open-ended approach, aligning our investments with the long-term growth aspirations of our partners. We understand the limitations of conventional buyout models and their potential to hinder the motivations of management and employees.

Our investment approach is highly flexible, allowing us to structure deals that cater to specific business stages. Whether it's a buyout, minority investment, business succession plan, or growth capital injection, we have the expertise to craft a solution that aligns with your company's objectives.

Our ambition extends beyond generating short-term returns. We aspire to be a catalyst for long-term economic growth by creating and supporting corporate groups that contribute positively to Japanese employment and the overall economy.

Our services encompass more than just capital provision. We offer hands-on management support, leveraging our members' wealth of experience and networks in areas such as overseas expansion, digital transformation (DX) adaptation, and talent acquisition. This multifaceted approach helps companies overcome challenges like market contraction, labor shortages, and succession planning, ultimately maximizing their long-term value.
